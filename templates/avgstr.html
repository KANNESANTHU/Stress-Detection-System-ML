Suggestions to manage Average stress :



  1. 1.Put on some music and dance around.
  2. 2.  Take your dog for a walk.
  3. 3.  Walk or cycle to the grocery store.
  4. 4.  Use the stairs at home or work rather than an elevator.
  5. 5.  Park your car in the farthest spot in the lot and walk the rest of the way.
  6. 6.  Pair up with an exercise partner and encourage each other as you work out.
  7. 7.  Play ping-pong or an activity-based video game with your kids.
